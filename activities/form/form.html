Year in School:

The form tag has an "action" attribute. What does it do?

It specifies the URL where the form data will be sent after submission.

The form tag has a "method" attribute. What does it do?

It defines how the form data is sent (GET or POST).

What is the difference between a "POST" and a "GET"?

GET: Sends data in the URL (visible, limited, cached).

POST: Sends data in the request body (hidden, larger, not cached).
