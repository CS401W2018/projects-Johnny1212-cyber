What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?

Most tags are block tags, which group elements together, such as <div>, <header>, <nav>, <main>, <footer>, and headings (h1-h6). In block tags, we can adjust width, height, margin, padding, and border, but in inline tags, we cannot. Examples of inline tags: <span>, <a>, <em>, <mark>, <img>.

What is the margin, border, and padding?

The outermost space outside of the box is called the margin. The boundary of the element is the border. The space inside the border, between the content and the border, is the padding.

What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?

Fixed units: px, in, cm. Font units: pt. Relative measurements: %, em (multiplies by a factor), rem.

When would you use a figure tag?

The <figure> tag is used to contain an image and allow text before and after it. It also allows us to add a caption below the image using the <figcaption> tag.
