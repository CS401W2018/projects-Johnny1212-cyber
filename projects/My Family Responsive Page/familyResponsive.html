Interesting Facts About the Duttons

  • The Duttons have owned the Yellowstone Ranch for generations.
  • John Dutton is fiercely protective of his land and family.
  • Beth Dutton is one of the most ruthless businesswomen in Montana.
  • Rip Wheeler is the enforcer of the family, ensuring loyalty at all costs.
  • The ranch is constantly under threat from developers and rivals.

Dutton Ranch

John Dutton continues to battle developers and politicians trying to take over the Yellowstone Ranch. With the help of his loyal ranch hands and family, he fights to maintain his legacy.

Beth and Rip

Beth Dutton and Rip Wheeler share a love built on years of loyalty and hardship. No matter the challenges they face, their connection remains one of the strongest forces on the ranch.

Dutton Family Conflict

The Dutton family is no stranger to internal power struggles. Jamie Dutton's ambitions often put him at odds with his father and sister, making the battle for control of the ranch even more intense.
