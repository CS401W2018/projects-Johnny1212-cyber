Food Spotlight: Curry Udon

Fun Facts About Curry Udon

  • A popular Japanese comfort food.
  • Combines thick udon noodles with a savory curry broth.
  • Often topped with green onions, tempura, or sliced meat.
  • Originated in Japan during the Meiji era.
  • Best enjoyed on a cold day for warmth and comfort.
